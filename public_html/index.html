    Elevate Your Horizon: Where Opulence Meets the Sky!

    Venkatesh Skydale stands as a pinnacle of luxury, situated on Sinhgad Road near Rajaram Bridge, meticulously crafted by Venkatesh Buildcon Pvt Ltd. This gated community project offers an enchanting panorama of Taljai Hills and Riverview, elevating the living experience for the residents of this housing society. Comprising four premium high-rise towers, Venkatesh Skydale presents a variety of configurations, including 2, 3, 4, and 5 BHK luxurious apartments. With over 40 ultra-luxury amenities, Skydale is designed to redefine the standards of opulent living.

    Positioned as a landmark project in the vicinity, Skydale boasts competitive pricing at Rajaram Bridge, Sinhagad Road. Its strategic location ensures excellent connectivity to city centers such as Swargate, Pune Station, Shivaji Nagar, Nal Stop, Karve Road, Kothrud, and Erandwane. The construction of Skydale will unfold in distinct phases—Phase 1, Phase 2, Phase 3, and Phase 4—with the latest possessions scheduled from March 2024 in accordance with RERA guidelines. This exceptional project encompasses a plethora of ultra-luxury amenities, including a club house, swimming pool, kids pool, kids play area, multipurpose court, gym, play aerobics and zumba hall, pathways, badminton courts, musical garden, business lounge, stepping stones, feature walls, cricket pitch, society office, Wi-Fi zone, buffet area, a vast and spacious parking area, and a library, among many others. Venkatesh Skydale ensures a lifestyle that epitomizes sophistication and comfort.
